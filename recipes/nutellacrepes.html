A Simple Recipe for Nutella Crepes

Description

Crepes are thin type of pancake. They're quick and easy to make and can be a sweet dessert or a savory treat. This recipe will teach you how to make a sweet nutella and strawberry crepe.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 cup flour
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • powdered sugar
  • nutella
  • strawberries

Preparation

  1. Grab a large bowl and whisk eggs, milk, butter, flour, sugar, salt, and vanilla extract together until batter is smooth.
  2. Place batter in fridge for an hour.
  3. Heat up a non-stick pan and coat with butter.
  4. Use a ladle to pour a small portion of the crepe batter into the pan and swirl batter to spread it evenly. Cook for 30 seconds and then flip. Cook for another 15 seconds then remove from pan. Repeat until battter is gone.
  5. Spread nutella onto crepe using butter knife and fold crepe. Add powdered sugar and cut up strawberries as desired.
