Chicken Parmesan Recipe

Description

Chicken parmesan is a classic italian dish, yet very easy for any beginner to make. You may have also heard this dish referred to as Chicken Parmigiana, but rest assured they are the same thing.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 4 slices mozzarella cheese or shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/2 cup olive oil

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees and apply non stick cooking spray to oven tray.
  2. Cut chicken breasts into halves.
  3. Place chicken breasts one by one between two sheets of plastic on a cutting board. Pound chicken with a meat mallet.
  4. Beat eggs in a bowls and whisk together with garlic, salt and pepper. One by one, coat chicken in the mixture one both sides. Set aside.
  5. Mix bread crumbs and parmesan cheese in a different shallow bowl. Dip chicken one by one into the mixture, making sure to thouroughly coat.
  6. Heat olive oil in a deep skillet and fry chicken until brown and crispy making sure to turn and cook evenly.
  7. Remove chicken from skillet and place on oven tray.
  8. Heat up marinara sauce and top chicken with it.
  9. Top chicken with mozzarella cheese
  10. Bake chicken in oven until cheese is melted and serve.
