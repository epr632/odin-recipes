Manicotti Recipe

Description

Another traditional Italian dish, Manicotti is a dish that is sure to become a family favorite. Fun fact, Manicotti means little sleeves in Italian!

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • Manicotti pasta
  • Mozarella cheese
  • 1 jar pasta sauce

Preparation

  1. Cook manicotti in boiling water until done but firm
  2. Chop spinach until minced
  3. Mix the pint of ricotta cheese,egg yolk, spinach,and parmesan cheese in bowl. Fill piping bag with mixture.
  4. Fill each manicotti with mixture using piping bag until 3/4 full.
  5. Pour 1/2 of the jar of pasta in a baking dish. Then place manicotti in dish. Pour the rest of the sauce over the manicotti and sprinkle parmesan cheese and mozarella cheese over top.
  6. Place baking dish in oven until cheese is melted and golden brown.
